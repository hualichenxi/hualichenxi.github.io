About me

Hi! I am Xi Chen. I got my Ph.D in College of Computer Science in Zhejiang University, with the supervision of Prof. Huajun Chen. In 2015, I spent several months as a visiting student at Prof. Calton Pu's lab in Georgia Tech University.

From 2015 to 2017, I was a research intern at Business Platform-Data Service Department of Alibaba (Product Knowledge Graph Group). From 2017 to 2018, I was a senior Researcher at Huawei Noah Lab. Currently, I am a senior researcher at Tencent Jarvis Lab (Leader of KG and NLP Team).

We are hiring interns and full-time employees on NLP, KG and medical AI. Feel free to send me (jasonxchen@tencent.com) your CV if you are interested.
